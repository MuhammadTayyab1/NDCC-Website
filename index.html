About NDCC

National Dialogue On Climate Change

Aims to bring back the environment to its natural course by identifying global environmental problems created by climate change and presenting eco-friendly solutions for a green future. It will be achieved by:

  • ✔️ Promoting research and development on climate change and its hazards.
  • ✔️Enlightening masses about the deterioration of the environment due to their harmful lifestyles.
  • ✔️Creating content to change public opinion in favor of eco-friendly development.
  • ✔️Educating multitudes about hazards, adaptation and mitigation of climate change through environment journalism.
  • ✔️Motivating youth, urban planners, engineers and all the stakeholders to contribute to sustainable development through their creativity and innovation.
  • ✔️Proposing policymakers and government official research-based solutions to move to greener and safer cities.
