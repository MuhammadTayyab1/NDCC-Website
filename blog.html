Nepal and Human Induced Climate Change:

I love nature. The peaceful feeling one gets when alone in natural environment is mesmerizing. Growing up, I always felt like the surrounding around me is changing. But wasn’t that exactly how it’s supposed to be? Nothing is constant and things that are now tangible may be intangible in the future, just like human beings we come across in our lives. But to what extent are we supposed to claim that this change is natural and that change isn’t? That’s where manmade changes come. According to Wikipedia, “The earliest roots of science can be traced to Ancient Egypt and Mesopotamia in around 3500 to 3000 BCE.” Now that is where we realize that we’ve come this far i.e. to 2020 A.D. and we have made some mistakes. Climate Change is a natural process but what happens when human beings are involved in it?
